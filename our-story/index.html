Our Story

From humble beginnings to a thriving, supportive community for minority migrant students on campus.

Our Mission

Did you know that over 60% of international students report feeling lonely or isolated during their studies? At muse.hub, We are dedicated to empowering minority migrant students through collaborative workspaces, skill-building workshops, and a supportive community. Our mission is to create an inclusive environment where everyone can thrive academically and personally.
