Welcome to muse. hub

Located on the UniMelb Parkville campus, our hub provides a collaborative, supportive and productive environment for our VA's and wider community.

VA Workspace & Trainings

muse.hub hosts professional training sessions for our VA's, quiet study spaces, and collaborative work areas designed to help our team grow their skills and boost productivity.

  • Virtual Assistant Trainings for our assistants
  • Dedicated coworking desks
  • Private meeting booths

Community Workshops

muse.hub hosts weekly interactive workshops led by mental health experts and student peers. Our workshops build confidence, connections, and practical knowledge, helping struggling migtant graduate students thrive in their studies and beyond.

  • Weekly peer-networking events
  • Executive Functioning Workshops
  • Melbourne cultural integration outings
