our mission

muse. is a non-profit virtual assistant agency with a social mission

To Empower

We are a WISE business. We employ disadvantaged and low-income international graduate students to provide them with employment, community and professional experience.

To Train

We provide world-class virtual assistant training programs to ensure high quality service across the board.

To Support

We reinvest our profits to support vulnerable, minority international university students through executive functioning workshops and peer support networks.

To Integrate

We utilise existing university and social enterprise ecosystems in Melbourne to build a sustainable, community-integrated business.
